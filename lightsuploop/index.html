This Privacy Policy describes how "Lightsup Loop" ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information Provided by Google Play Games Services

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, we may access your:

  • Public Profile Name
  • Player ID
  • Game Progress (via Cloud Save/Snapshots)
  • Achievement and Leaderboard data

B. Analytical Data

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This includes data such as:

  • Number of levels completed
  • Time spent in the App
  • In-app events (e.g., theme changes, hints used)
  • Device information (model, OS version)

C. Crash Data

We use Firebase Crashlytics to monitor and fix App stability issues. This collects technical details about app crashes.

D. Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized advertising, including:

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Interaction with ads

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving the game experience.
  • Synchronizing game progress across devices.
  • Managing achievements and leaderboards.
  • Analyzing app performance and fixing bugs.
  • Delivering relevant advertisements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information with third-party service providers (Google/Firebase) only as necessary to provide the services described above.

4. In-App Purchases

All financial transactions for "Remove Ads" are handled securely by the Google Play Billing System. We do not collect or store your credit card information.

5. Your Choices

  • Play Games: You can choose not to sign in to Play Games.
  • Ads: You can disable personalized ads in your Android device settings or purchase the "Remove Ads" feature to stop seeing advertisements entirely.
